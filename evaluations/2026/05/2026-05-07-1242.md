**Confidence:** Low

### Observation

For the CFO, this matters because (1) if small-cap credit conditions are improving, it suggests regional banks (which lend heavily to small caps) are easing standards, which is a leading indicator for broader credit availability, and (2) if we compete with or supply to small-cap companies, their liquidity position affects our receivables risk and pricing power.
