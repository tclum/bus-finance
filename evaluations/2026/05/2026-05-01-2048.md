A 25–50 bps upward revision to the terminal rate would compress tech multiples by 5–10% and rotate flows into financials and energy—neither of which led today (XLF -0.40%, XLE -1.34%). The CFO needs to know whether our own debt refinancing timeline (if any maturities are in the next 12 months) should be accelerated before the curve reprices, and whether our equity portfolio tilt toward tech needs rebalancing.

### Recommended investigation
